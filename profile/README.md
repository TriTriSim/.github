# TriTriSim

TriTriSim is a software company and development group founded by Triantafyllos Kassios and Pedro Szabo. The team is dedicated to creating high-fidelity add-ons and tools for Microsoft Flight Simulator (2020 and 2024), focusing on the development of advanced atmospheric and visual effects.

## TFX - Emergency Effects

TFX is a tool designed to visually simulate critical situations and emergency events. The objective of the project is to provide greater realism during failure training and simulated flights.

### Features
* Visual modeling of engine fires and electrical sparks.
* Implementation of smoke trails, water splashes, and landing gear failures.
* Impact and crash animations.

### Compatibility
* Simulators: Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020 and 2024.
* Aircraft: PMDG 777 series, Fenix A320 series, FlyByWire A320/A380, iFly 737 Max 8, iniBuilds A300 and A350, among others.

## GFX - General Flight Effects

GFX is an immersion add-on designed to enhance atmospheric and operational visual effects for airliners. It utilizes a custom mathematical framework that processes validated simulation data to determine precise atmospheric responses where default simulator values may lack accuracy.

### Features
* Wing condensation and engine vortices.
* Thrust-on-rain interaction, water ingestion, and water trails.
* Advanced dynamic contrail generation.

### Compatibility
* Simulators: Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020 and 2024.
* Aircraft: Broad support for airliner families, including models from PMDG, Fenix, FlyByWire, iniBuilds, ToLiss, and TFDi.
